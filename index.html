About

RECORDING

Recording is the first step towards achieving your final product. With me, your sessions are tracked in a professional, home-based recording studio with a comfortable working environment. I traditionally track on a Pro Tools HD3 System, but can accommodate to other platforms/Digital audio workstations if need be.

GEAR LISTING

I record, mix, and master completely ITB (In the box), alongside a collection of analog gear.

MIXING

Mixing is an important part of the recording process. This is the point where I take all of the elements that you've spent so much time laying down, and blend them into one balanced sound. I offer mixing that best fits your sound and is unique to each project.

I care about your project just as much as you do. With that said, I may not always be able to get the perfect mix the first time. For this reason, you will receive my initial mix, and up to two revised mixes. Your input is just as important as mine, so let's work together to achieve a great mix.

MIXING INCLUDES
1) MIXING OF TRACK AND/OR ALBUM 2) HIGH QUALITY MP3 FORMAT AUDIO FILE
3) TWO REVISED REMIXES 4) ACAPELLA VERSION OF TRACK
5) HIGH QUALITY WAV FORMAT AUDIO FILE 6) INSTRUMENTAL VERSION OF TRACK
Large Button

MASTERING

Mastering puts the final touches on your project. This is where we boost the energy of your mixes, stabilize all frequencies, create unity between all tracks and ensure that the sound and feel of your mixes translatse effectively through any listening platform. An additional bonus provided by mastering is that your tracks will be loud enough for commercial standards!

MASTERING INCLUDES
1) PROFESSIONAL MASTERING OF TRACK AND/OR ALBUM 2) DDP FILES FOR DUPLICATION
3) HIGH QUALITY WAV FORMAT AUDIO FILE. 4) TAGGING OF ALBUM ARTWORK
5) HIGH QUALITY DIGITAL RELEASE READY MP3 6) PROPER SPACING & FADING OF TRACKS ON ALBUM/EP
